The Daily Kitchen Dilemma

We've all been there. Two major problems that make cooking feel like a chore.

🤯 What to Cook?

Too many recipe choices lead to decision paralysis. This 'choice overload' is real and often results in us cooking the same old meals or ordering takeout.

🗑️ Wasted Groceries

Globally, about one-third of all food produced is wasted. The ingredients we buy often wilt and spoil before we get a chance to use them. A waste of money and resources.
